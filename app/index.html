How does this work?

With the introduction of new APIs available to the user agent, our childish minds noticed a certain theme that could be misconstrued for something with completely different benefits.

Users can experience a completely different interface with technology. Mobile devices will become truly smart. It gives a whole new meaning to the phrase "user-friendly".

It was time to get under the hood of this new technology and put it to good use.
